WHAT WE OFFER YOU

Premium Listing

Sellers can pay to have their items featured prominently on the homepage or at the top of search results. This premium feature increases visibility and gives the items more exposure to potential buyers, helping them sell faster.

Analytics Dashboard

Provide sellers with an analytics tool that tracks the performance of their listings. They can see how many people view their items, how often they click on them, and how long they stay on the page. This data helps sellers optimize their listings to improve sales.

Discount Tools

Allow sellers to create special offers, such as time-limited discounts or bundle deals. They can offer percentage-based discounts or promotions for multiple purchases, which encourages more buyers and drives up sales.
